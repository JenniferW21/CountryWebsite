Cuisine

Spring Rolls

春卷

Spring Rolls are eaten as an appetizer.
These rolls are filled with vegetables and meats.
They are mainly eaten during the Spring Festival.

Chow Mein

炒面

Chow Mein is a traditional Chinese noodle dish
Chow Mein is stir-fried noodles with vegetables.
It is made with a hot wok, accompained by many ingredients.
They are a popular dish made in China .

HotPot

火锅

Chinese hot pot is a dish shared with everyone.
Hot pot is a flavorful broth with meats and vegetables.
The Chinese name for hot pot is 'huo guo'.

You tiao

油条

Youtiao was orginated from the Southern Song dynasty.
Youtiao is a golden deep fried wheat flour dough.
Youtiao is a popular Chinese breakfast food.
It is usually eaten with congee or soy milk
